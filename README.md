# Canberra-Build-Guild-Badges

PCB badges made for Build Guild Canberra. PCB's consist of a push button, LED, and resistor. Attendees will be soldering these and keeping them.

Quite an easy project to make but the idea took a while. Originally it was going to be a 555 chaser but I really wanted to focus on the simplest circuitry ever and make soldering easy.

## Assembly Instructions

To assemble you simple need to solder all tht components in their marked spots. The two testpoints on the bottom left of the board are for connecting flyends of a 9v battery snap, the red wire is positive and black is negative. Don't mess up the polaritiy!

## Materials Needed
- 1x PCB (This!)
- 1x 7mm push switch
- 1x 390 ohm resistor
- 1x LED
- 1x 9v Battery
- 1x 9v Battery snap connector
